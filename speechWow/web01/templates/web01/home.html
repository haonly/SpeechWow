Why we do what we do?

Public speaking is one of the biggest fears people have. In fact, one researchstudy in The Journal of Education and Educational Development (Raja, 2017) showed that people “who fear public speaking can perform well if they use certain strategies to fight their fears. 75% of participants admitted their fear of public speaking and 95% participants agreed that if proper counseling, instruction and coaching is provided, this fear can be overcome,” and that is what we can do for you and why we are here. No fear of public speaking? We can still help take your skills to the next level!

How can you benefit?

Confidence! Our evaluation teams are here to help take your presentation confidence to the next level. If you are like most people, you likely practice your presentations in front of a mirror and that is certainly better than no practice at all. However, how about practicing in front of a mirror, then practicing in front of a group of three highly-qualified evaluators who will provide you written feedback within three hours of finishing your presentation? Would that help take your next presentation to the next level? Of course! Increased confidence will lead to increased opportunities!

Why we do what we do?

Our evaluators are native English-speaking teachers from America, Canada, England, Australia, Scotland, and South America. They all have extensive experience in public-speaking instruction and have completed SpeechWow’sintensive training program. They are especially skilled at delivering practical, positive, and meaningful feedback to help you Wow your next audience and every audience after that!
